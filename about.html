About Our Mission

Start-Art is an Organization for Young/Upcoming Artists who need a professional hand to guide them into taking their first steps to success. They help with creating your Art portfolio, finding new job opportunities, and connections in the art field you are looking into. They will also provide courses on professional art environments and help you develop the necessary skills for that field ( Building a proposal, presenting ideas, Etc.)

Always looking for new artists, Start-Art will often partner with colleges in the area to pre-emptively connect with college students looking to start art careers. They also partner with many companies that rely on them to provide good, trustworthy hires for their company. In perspective, they create connections in the community through art and good work experience.
