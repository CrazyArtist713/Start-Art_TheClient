Overview/Mission

Located in Eau Claire, WI, Start-Art aims to create a platform for young adults who want to take their first steps into the professional world with their art. Their goal is to assist students and those seeking work in creating art portfolios and walking them through the application process. They do this by giving tips and tricks as well as creating a platform to more easily build and construct your art portfolio.
